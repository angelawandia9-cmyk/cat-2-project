News & Stories

What's brewing at Zuri Café

June 2, 2026
We're Now Roasting In-House

After months of planning, we've installed our own small-batch roaster. This means fresher beans, more control over flavour profiles, and the ability to experiment with new origins from across Kenya.

May 18, 2026
Open Mic Night Returns Every Friday

By popular demand, our monthly open mic is now a weekly Friday evening event. Local poets, singers, and musicians are welcome to sign up at the counter from 5pm onward.

April 30, 2026
New Partnership with Nyeri Farmers

We've signed a direct-trade agreement with a smallholder cooperative in Nyeri, guaranteeing fair prices and giving you an even fresher cup, harvest to brew in under six weeks.

April 10, 2026
Introducing Our New Lunch Menu

Sandwiches, salads, and a daily pie special have joined our menu, giving regulars a reason to swing by for lunch, not just their morning coffee.

March 22, 2026
Zuri Café Turns 7 Years Old

From a four-seat kiosk on Waiyaki Way to a full café in Westlands, thank you Nairobi for seven wonderful years. We celebrated with free pastries for every customer all day.

March 5, 2026
Now Fully Plastic-Straw Free

As part of our sustainability commitment, we've switched entirely to paper straws and compostable takeaway cups across all our locations.
